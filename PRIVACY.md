# Privacy Policy for Bulan Buka

**Last updated: May 25, 2026**

## Overview

Bulan Buka ("we", "our", or "the app") is a multi-madhab Islamic prayer times application created by Legacy Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

**Bulan Buka is designed to work offline and respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

## Information We Access

### Location Data
Bulan Buka uses your device location **only on your device** to:
- Calculate accurate prayer times for your geographic position
- Determine the Qibla direction (toward the Kaaba in Mecca)

Your location is **never transmitted, stored on any server, or shared with third parties**. It is processed locally on your device and used only during the active session.

### Device Sensors
The app uses your device magnetometer and accelerometer sensors **only on your device** to provide an accurate Qibla compass. No sensor data is collected or shared.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Account information (no account is required)
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Crash reports or telemetry
- Contacts, photos, or other personal content
- Advertising identifiers

## Local Storage

Bulan Buka stores the following data **only on your device** using standard application storage:
- Your selected madhab (school of jurisprudence) and calculation method
- Notification preferences
- Tasbih counter history
- Language preference
- Theme preference

This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

## Notifications

Bulan Buka schedules **local notifications** on your device for prayer times. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or push notification services.

## Third-Party Services

Bulan Buka does **not** integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Bulan Buka does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated revision date.
